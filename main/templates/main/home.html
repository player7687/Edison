HOW IT WORK

UPLOAD YOUR MUSIC

Upload your songs to our service via drag and drop or via URL, wait for the service to analyze the musical work. The process takes from 15 seconds to a minute. Be patient while our system processes your favorite songs

SELECT UPLOADED MUSIC

After the song has been uploaded, our system will show the parameters of the song (eg song title, song's album, music genre, length, BPM, ...) and other parameters. Other technical parameters are also analyzed by the service (Spectograms, MFCCs, ...). You can choose to see the lyrics or listen to the song.

CHECK YOUR RECOMMENDATION

The highlight of our system is to provide you recommendations about songs that are similar to the ones you upload. Using the parameters analyzed and our optimized algorithms, the system can give you the closest suggestion of songs, which can save you tons of time in finding new interesting music.
